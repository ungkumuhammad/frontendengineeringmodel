    Disclaimer. This is an engineering screening tool. It computes a levelized cost from user inputs and documented benchmark defaults; it does not constitute financial advice or a vendor quotation. Upstream (well-to-gate) fuel emissions, tax credits (e.g. 45V/45Q), grid charges, insurance, decommissioning and financing structure beyond a single discount rate are out of scope unless folded into the editable inputs.
